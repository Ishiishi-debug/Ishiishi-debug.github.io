Technical Highlights

Magnetic Coin System

Swap the red fabric strap for a magnetic charger in seconds. The coin sits flush, ejected by a recessed button.

1TB Internal Storage

No more lost SD cards. Built‑in storage keeps your footage safe and simplifies your workflow.

Edge‑to‑Edge OLED

Front and back displays disappear into the anodised aluminium body – bright, sharp, and intuitive.

Tactile Controls

iMac M4‑inspired power button, pill‑shaped volume keys (front up / back down), and a dedicated capture button.

Premium Materials

Matte silver anodised aluminium with deep red accents. Compact dimensions: 65 × 48.75 × 23.83 mm.

Action‑Ready

Designed for adventure – rugged yet refined. Water‑resistant concept with a sapphire glass lens cover.

From Idea to Reality

I started with zero engineering knowledge, a 3D printer, and a stubborn curiosity. I taught myself Fusion 360 by crashing it more times than I can count. Every fillet, every button, every decision came from asking “what if?”

What if the strap was bright red fabric against cold silver metal? What if the buttons felt as good as they looked? What if the camera had 1TB internal storage so you never lose an SD card?

Inspired by Jony Ive’s Ferrari Luce interior, the iPhone 17 Pro’s anodised finish, and the iMac M4’s perfect power button, I built a camera that feels as good in your hand as it looks on paper.

Now it exists as a complete 3D model, technical drawings, and 3D‑printed prototypes (PLA‑CF and red PLA). This is version one – and it's only the beginning.
